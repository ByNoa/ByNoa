Sound Catcher

Sound Catcher
2025 - 2026

App Design UX/UI

Sound Catcher turns everyday inspiration into music by listening to a song, analyzing it, and instantly creating a score for guitar, piano, or bass. It’s your personal ear-to-sheet-music assistant.

  • Problem

    Access to musical sheets is often limited and restrictive. Many musicians struggle to find scores online for specific songs or independent artists. Musical sheets are often incomplete or unavailable in the web, making learning and playing music inaccessible.

  • Insight

    Musicians learn by listening. Through capturing and analyzing sounds and songs around you, it becomes possible to turn listening into an active creative tool rather than a passive experience.

  • Solution

    Sound Catcher is a mobile app that records and analyzes sounds around you (concerts, radio, TV or everyday surroundings) and transforms them into musical scores adapted to the user’s instrument and skill level. Users can create their personalized profiles, store their generated sheet music in a digital notebook, set their favorite artists and genres, and connect with other users.

  • Have you ever listened to a song and you could not find its score?

  • Transcribe every sound you want now!

  • The sounds you transcribe will be saved in your notebook

  • Read them and play them when you want!

  • Set your main instrument, your music taste and make musical friends
