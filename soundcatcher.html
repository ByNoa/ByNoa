Sound Catcher

Sound Catcher  ·  2025 – 2026

App Design, UX/UI

Sound Catcher turns everyday inspiration into music by listening to a song, analyzing it, and instantly creating a score for guitar, piano, or bass. It's your personal ear-to-sheet-music assistant.

Problem

Access to musical sheets is often limited and restrictive. Many musicians struggle to find scores online for specific songs or independent artists. Musical sheets are often incomplete or unavailable on the web, making learning and playing music inaccessible.

Insight

Musicians learn by listening. Through capturing and analyzing sounds and songs around you, it becomes possible to turn listening into an active creative tool rather than a passive experience.

Solution

Sound Catcher is a mobile app that records and analyzes sounds around you (concerts, radio, TV or everyday surroundings) and transforms them into musical scores adapted to the user's instrument and skill level. Users can create personalized profiles, store generated sheet music in a digital notebook, set favorite artists and genres, and connect with other users.
