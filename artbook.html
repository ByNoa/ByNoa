ArtBook

ArtBook  ·  2023 – 2025

Art  ·  Sketches, Line Art & Final Result

The Art of Noa Aguilera is an illustrated artbook that reveals my full creative process — from concept and sketch to final colours, lighting, and effects. It also features original character designs from three of my story projects, including Framing the Stars, a tale I wrote about Wanda, an astronaut searching for a true home. Her journey leads her to Mirabilia, a mysterious planet cursed by human fears. As she helps heal its strange inhabitants, she discovers the meaning of belonging, empathy, and inner growth. This project combines illustration and storytelling to reflect my vision as both an artist and a writer.
