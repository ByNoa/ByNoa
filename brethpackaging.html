Brēth Packaging

Brēth Packaging  ·  2025 – 2026

Packaging Design, Product Design, Editorial, Branding & Social Networks

Brēth is a speculative packaging design project in a dystopian future where humans must cultivate oxygen-producing plants within their homes to survive. Ferns were chosen for their high oxygen output, ease of care, cultivation and reproduction, making them ideal for domestic survival. The packaging includes a custom container shaped like the brand's logo, designed for growing and multiplying the ferns. It features an integrated natural-light system, activated by a built-in button. The packaging set also includes a ripe fern, moisture dispenser and a daily care guide. This speculative project combines branding, packaging, product and editorial design developed using Illustrator, Indesign, Photoshop and Blender.

When air becomes a resource

In a near future where oxygen levels drop and air quality at home defines our health, survival becomes a daily practice for humans.

This video presents a speculative data scenario set in a dystopian future where oxygen scarcity, low VO2 levels and poor air quality at home impact daily life.

Brēth is a domestic solution that reintroduces oxygen generation at home, transforming plant care (growing and multiplying ferns) into a vital health system. Growing oxygen at home is no longer a choice, but a necessity.

Fern's Flowerpot

The packaging includes a custom container shaped like the brand's logo, designed for growing and multiplying the ferns. It features an integrated indirect and soft lighting system. It also contains an irrigation system at the top for daily misting. This increases ambient humidity and maintains it above 60%. The flowerpot has good drainage to keep ferns healthy. The material used for the flowerpot's production is plastic — the best material for retaining moisture.
