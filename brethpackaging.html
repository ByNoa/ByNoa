Brēth Packaging

Brēth Packaging2025 - 2026

Packaging Design, Product Design, Editorial, Branding & Social Networks

Brēthis a speculative packaging design project in a dystopian future where humans must cultivate oxygen-producing plants within their homes to survive. Ferns were chosen for their high oxygen output, ease of care, cultivation and reproduction, making them ideal for domestic survival. The packaging includes a custom container shaped like the brand’s logo, designed for growing and multiplying the ferns. It features an integrated natural-light system, activated by a built-in button. The packaging set also includes a ripe fern, moisure dispenser and a daily care guide. This speculative project combines branding, packaging, product and editorial design developed using Illustrator, Indesign, Photoshop and Blender.

When air becomes a resource

In a near future where oxygen levels drop and air quality at home defines our health, survival becomes a daily practice for humans.

This video presents a speculative data scenario set in a dystopian future where oxygen scarcity, low VO2 levels and poor air quality at home impact daily life.

Brēth is a domestic solution that reintroduces oxygen generation at home, transforming plant care (growing and multiplying ferns) into a vital health system. Growing oxygen at home is no longer a choice, but a necessity.
