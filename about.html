About me

I'm Noa Aguilera, a designer and visual storyteller shaped by curiosity and trained at EINA (UAB, Barcelona). For as long as I can remember, I've observed the world through textures, light, and quiet moments. Design became the natural space where I could turn feeling into form.


I began my path collaborating with creative agencies, learning to build strong visual identities. Now, I focus on digital design—web, social media, newsletters, and visual storytelling. Each project is a dialogue between concept and emotion, structure and spontaneity.


I believe in creating with intention, crafting experiences that feel human and resonant. Design, for me, is a poetic language—gentle, precise, and deeply expressive. It bridges the seen and the felt, the idea and the heart. Through it, I connect—with people, with purpose, and with meaning.
