Five Senses2019 - 2020

Editorial Design

Five Sensesis a magazine editorial design which talks about Bompass and Parr; Two designers well known for their unique, innovative and transgressive projects of food design, experimental design, sensitive design and sound design, among others. They are also known for organizing shows and events that revolve around their design projects in London. Each chapter of the magazine talks about different ways in which their designs work and they are accompanied by typographic editions, color and paper treatments that complement it.

Catalogue Automation

This project could look like a “magic trick” but it is catalogue automation. The working experience in this field allows to create magazines, catalogues, books and other editorial projects with multiple products, modules and automate them in seconds.
