PhotoBook2018 - 2025

Photography, Retouch & Editorial

Keane, The Art of The Eye, a photographic project divided into different chapters. Each one is characterized by having a different tone but they are all part of the same photographic style. They are known by being transgressors but without forgetting the natural, spiritual and feminine touch. These chapters are differentiated by their subject matter and are accompanied by poetic texts.
